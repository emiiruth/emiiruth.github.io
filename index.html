YOUTUBER COMMENT GHOSTWRITING

Fall 2020 - Summer 2021

For me, ghostwriting was an exercise in leaving behind my own identity and taking on a new voice. Learning to bend language to my will for expression so far outside of my own style gave me better control of my prose, deepened my understanding of a demographic I once found foreign (middle aged white women who love interior design), and instilled an acute understanding that the voices we consume on social media may not be exactly what they seem.

LIFETIME CONCERT HISTORY

2013 - Current

In 2013, my dad surprised me with my first ever concert: a Christian rock show in an honest to god (lol) church! The rest is history - I've been taking myself to as many shows as possible ever since (although I haven't seen any legitimate Christian rock since 2013 which is regrettable). Before you judge my choices, keep in mind that I was a (young) 13 in 2013, and thusly 14 and 2014, 15 in 2015, etc. I recognize that going from Neutral Milk Hotel to the Chainsmokers to Hoodie Allen to the Decemberists is insane - my only defense is that puberty is a confusing time for all of us.
